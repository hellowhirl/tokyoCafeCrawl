Atmosphere

Story

ストーリー

The name of the lounge “Unplan” literally means “not planned.” For people that are staying at the hostel they can offer suggestions on places to go in Tokyo. The owner of Unplan Lounge Mr. used to take trips overseas and could usually get information about sightseeing from tourist centers. But in Japan he thinks there are not enough information centers for foreign tourists who come the “unplanned” route. Unplan Lounge is a great place to talk, drink, and just hang out. After speaking with friendly staff they can offer suggestions on where to go based on the conversations that take place. Coffee is excellent and the bar staff are confident to serve you a good drink. No matter where you come from everyone can feel at home while away from home here. It’s better for travelers to feel at home. The lounge shares the same space as the front desk for the hostel so there are lots of free information brochures and magazines about Tokyo. There is also a good chance to meet people from all over the world. He especially likes it when travelers mingle and to talk with local people.

『アンプラン』とは計画を立てずに行動すること。プランなしの外国人旅行者はこちらのカフェで色んな東京観光の計画を立てることができます。東京は観光案内所が少ないためこちらではたくさんのパンフレットや地図を置き様々な観光名所を紹介しています。こちらのラウンジは楽に過ごせる無国籍なイメージ。スタッフは英語も話せるため気軽に話しかけやすく、たくさんの情報提供をしてくれます。コーヒーはもちろん、カクテルも美味しいです。ラウンジの中にホステルのチェックインカウンターがあるため、ラウンジで楽しんでいる様々な国の人たちとすぐに親しくなれます。オーナーは人と人とのつながりを大切にし、観光客と互いの文化や伝統を交換し合う国際交流の場にしたいと思っています。
