Story

ストーリー

This café has a long history and continues to attract customers from within Japan and around the world. Local Tokyoites and even Japanese celebrities like to frequent this historic café. After the Tokyo fires from the Second World War were extinguished this building was raised from an area where there was nothing. Since then the interior has gone through some changes but has endured with its classic structure throughout. In the past it was a gathering place for novelists and people in publishing/newspapers.

コーヒーにこだわり、日本人のみならず海外の観光客にも大人気です。1946年に創業以来、昭和の風情が残るイメージを保ってきているカフェです。昔の昭和のテイストを残しながらもリニューアルしている感じが素晴らしいです。昭和の有名な芸術家たちも訪れ歴史の深いお店です。
